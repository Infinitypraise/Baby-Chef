Eba

Ingredients

  • Garri (cassava flakes)
  • Hot water

How to prepare:

  1. Boil clean water in a kettle or pot until it reaches a rolling boil.
  2. Pour the hot water into a bowl.
  3. Gradually sprinkle garri into the hot water while stirring continuously with a wooden spatula.
  4. Keep stirring until the mixture thickens and forms a smooth, stretchy dough.
  5. Cover and allow to steam for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Stir again and mold into desired shapes for serving.
