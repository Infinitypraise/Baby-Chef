Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • Banana (ripe)
  • Evaporated milk or can milk
  • Yogurt
  • Honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Equipments

  • Blender
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Glass cup (for serving)

How to prepare:

  1. Peel and slice the bananas using a knife and cutting board.
  2. Add the banana slices, milk, yogurt, and honey (if using) into the blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add ice cubes and blend again if you want it chilled.
  5. Pour into a glass cup and serve immediately.

Enjoy with (optional):

  • Cookies
  • Granola
  • Fresh fruits
  • Salad
