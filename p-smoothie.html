Pineapple Smoothie

Ingredients

  • Fresh pineapple (peeled and chopped)
  • Banana (optional)
  • Yogurt or milk (optional)
  • Honey or sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes
  • Lime or lemon juice (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Wash and peel the pineapple, then cut into small chunks.
  2. Place pineapple chunks in a blender. Add banana, yogurt or milk, and honey if desired.
  3. Add ice cubes and a splash of lime or lemon juice for extra freshness.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  5. Taste and adjust sweetness or thickness as needed.
  6. Pour into cups and serve immediately, chilled.

Proteins (optional):

  • Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Chia seeds
  • Nuts (cashew, almond, walnut)

Also with a combo of (optional):

  • Table water
  • Chapman
  • Banana smoothies
  • Fruit salad
