Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • Fresh tomatoes or canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste (optional)
  • Onions
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Carrot (optional)
  • Vegetable or chicken stock
  • Olive oil or vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • Cream or milk (optional)
  • Basil or parsley (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Wash and chop tomatoes, onions, garlic, and carrot.
  2. Heat oil in a pot, add onions and garlic, saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and carrot, cook for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add tomato paste (if using), stir well.
  5. Pour in vegetable or chicken stock, add salt, pepper, and sugar to taste.
  6. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until vegetables are soft.
  7. Blend the soup until smooth (use a blender or immersion blender).
  8. Return to pot, add cream or milk if desired, and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Garnish with basil or parsley and serve hot.
