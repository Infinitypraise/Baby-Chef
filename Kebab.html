Kebab

Ingredients

  • Beef, chicken, or lamb (cut into cubes)
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes (optional)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika or chili powder
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Ginger (optional)
  • Skewers (wooden or metal)

How to prepare:

  1. Soak wooden skewers in water for 30 minutes if using.
  2. Marinate meat cubes with oil, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic, and ginger for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Chop bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Thread meat and vegetables alternately onto skewers.
  5. Preheat grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  6. Grill kebabs for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until meat is cooked and veggies are slightly charred.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite dips or sides.

Proteins:

  • Beef
  • Chicken
  • Lamb
  • Fish (optional)
  • Paneer or tofu (vegetarian option)

  • Table water
