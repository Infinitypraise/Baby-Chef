Milanesa

Ingredients

  • Thin beef or chicken cutlets
  • Eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder (optional)
  • Parsley (optional)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

How to prepare:

  1. Pound the meat cutlets thin if needed. Season with salt, pepper, and optional garlic powder or parsley.
  2. Beat eggs in a bowl. Place bread crumbs in another bowl.
  3. Dip each cutlet in the beaten eggs, then coat with bread crumbs, pressing gently so crumbs stick well.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Fry the breaded cutlets until golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  6. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve hot with lemon wedges and your favorite sides.

  • Fish (optional)
