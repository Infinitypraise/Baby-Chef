Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (drumsticks, thighs, or whole)
  • Onions
  • Scotch bonnet or chili pepper (optional)
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Ginger (optional)
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Water

How to prepare:

  1. Wash chicken pieces thoroughly and place in a pot.
  2. Add chopped onions, scotch bonnet, garlic, ginger, curry powder, thyme, seasoning cubes, and salt.
  3. Add a little water, cover, and allow to steam on medium heat for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Pour in more water to cover the chicken, then cook until chicken is tender.
  5. Remove chicken pieces and set aside the stock for rice or other dishes.
  6. Heat vegetable oil in a pan and fry the chicken pieces until golden brown (optional).
  7. Serve hot as a main dish or as a side with rice, yam, or plantain.

Pairs well with:

  • Jollof Rice
  • Coconut Rice
  • Fried Rice
  • Yam
  • Plantain

Also with a combo of (optional):

  • Table water
  • Soft Drinks
  • Chapman
  • Banana smoothies
  • Salad
