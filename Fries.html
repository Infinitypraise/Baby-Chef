French Fries

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt
  • Paprika or black pepper (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Peel potatoes and cut into thin strips or sticks.
  2. Rinse the cut potatoes in cold water to remove excess starch.
  3. Soak the potatoes in cold water for 30 minutes, then drain and pat dry with a clean towel.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 175°C (350°F).
  5. Fry the potatoes in batches until golden and crispy, about 3-5 minutes per batch.
  6. Remove fries and drain on paper towels.
  7. Season immediately with salt and optional paprika or black pepper.
  8. Serve hot with ketchup, mayonnaise, or your favorite dip.
