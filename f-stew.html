Fried Stew

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes (fresh or canned)
  • Red bell pepper or cayenne pepper
  • Scotch bonnet
  • Onions
  • Vegetable oil
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Chicken, beef, or fish (pre-cooked)
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Ginger (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Blend tomatoes, red bell pepper, scotch bonnet, onions, garlic, and ginger until smooth.
  2. Pour the blended mixture into a pot and boil until most of the water evaporates.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan. Add chopped onions and fry until translucent.
  4. Add the boiled tomato mixture and fry, stirring often, until the oil floats to the top and the stew is well reduced.
  5. Add curry powder, thyme, seasoning cubes, and salt to taste. Stir well.
  6. Add pre-cooked chicken, beef, or fish. Mix and allow to simmer for 10-15 minutes so the protein absorbs the stew flavor.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve hot with rice, yam, plantain, or any preferred side.
