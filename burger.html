Burger

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion, peeled and finely chopped
  • Beef steak mince
  • Mixed dried herbs
  • Egg, beaten
  • Cheddar (optional)
  • White rolls
  • Few round lettuce leaves, torn
  • Beef tomato, sliced
  • Ketchup, to serve (optional)

Equipments

  • Bowl
  • Frying pan
  • Barbecue or griddle
  • Plate (for serving)

How to prepare:

  1. In a bowl, mix the beef steak mince, chopped onion, mixed dried herbs, and beaten egg. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Shape the mixture into burger patties.
  3. Heat olive oil in a frying pan, barbecue, or griddle over medium heat.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
  5. If using cheddar, place a slice on each patty and let it melt.
  6. Slice the white rolls and lightly toast them if desired.
  7. Assemble the burger: place lettuce leaves, tomato slices, and the cooked patty in the roll. Add ketchup if desired.
  8. Serve hot on a plate.

Enjoy with (optional):

  • Fries
  • Salad
  • Banana smoothie
  • Soft drinks
