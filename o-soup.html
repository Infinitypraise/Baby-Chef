Okra Soup

Ingredients

  • Fresh okra (sliced or grated)
  • Palm oil
  • Beef, goat meat, or assorted meats
  • Stock fish (optional)
  • Dry fish (optional)
  • Crayfish (ground)
  • Onions
  • Scotch bonnet or chili pepper
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Vegetable leaves (ugu, spinach, or bitterleaf, optional)
  • Water

How to prepare:

  1. Wash and season your meat and fish with onions, seasoning cubes, salt, and pepper. Boil until tender and set aside, reserving the stock.
  2. Heat palm oil in a pot, add chopped onions and fry until translucent.
  3. Add ground crayfish and pepper, stir for a minute.
  4. Add the cooked meat, stock fish, and dry fish. Pour in the reserved stock and a little water if needed.
  5. Bring to a boil, then add the sliced or grated okra. Stir well.
  6. Allow to cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally until okra is soft and soup is slightly thickened.
  7. Add your washed and chopped vegetable leaves (if using). Stir and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Adjust salt and seasoning if needed. Serve hot.
