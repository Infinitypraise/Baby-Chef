Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Green peas
  • Sweet corn
  • Green beans
  • Bell pepper
  • Onions
  • Spring onions (optional)
  • Chicken or beef stock
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Vegetable oil
  • Liver (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Wash 4 cups of rice thoroughly with cold water and drain.
  2. Parboil rice in boiling water for about 10 minutes, drain and set aside.
  3. In a pot, add chicken or beef stock, seasoning cubes, curry powder, thyme, and salt. Bring to a boil.
  4. Add the parboiled rice to the stock and cook until the liquid is absorbed and rice is almost done.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok. Add chopped onions and fry until translucent.
  6. Add diced carrots, green beans, bell pepper, sweet corn, green peas, and liver (if using). Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add the cooked rice in batches, stirring and mixing well with the vegetables.
  8. Continue to stir-fry until everything is well combined and rice is fully cooked.
  9. Add chopped spring onions (optional) and mix.
  10. Serve hot with your choice of protein.
