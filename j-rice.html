Jolof Rice

Ingridients

  • Rice
  • Scotch bonnet
  • Tomatoes
  • Cayenne pepper or red bell pepper
  • Sachet or canned tomatoes
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Steamed Seasoned Chicken or fish water
  • Onions
  • Crayfish ( optional)
  • Hot dog

How to prepare:

  1. Fetch rice of 4 cups of empty 150g tin .
  2. Wash the rice with cold water and drain.
  3. Get a clean pot,place on heat add 1.5 liter water and add rice , allow to boil for 25mins parboiling.
  4. Wash again with cold water and drain rice put back on heat with little salt ( 1.05 tea spoons ) and 750ml hot water allow to boil until the water is dried for 20 more min.
  5. Remove from fire and pour in a basket to drain
  6. Make a red base sauce:
      Blend;
    • Red hot pepper 20 ounces
    • Tomatoes 3 ounces
    • Bell or cayenne 3 ounces
    • 1 red Onions
  7. Drain the water with tiny kitchen filter net
  8. Place pot on heat add 750g vegetable oil to heat
  9. Toast shopped onion 1/2 ball
  10. Add the blended pepper
  11. Add chicken sauce water of about 400ml ( optional)
  12. Add seasoning to taste
  13. Leave for about 5 mins
  14. Add bayleaf
  15. Add seasoning , salt , thyme to taste
  16. Allow it to boil to hundred degrees
  17. Pour your rice and steer evenly
  18. Allow to dry
  19. Add chopped; ( optional)
    • Carrot
    • Bell pepper
    • Onions
    • Sweet corn
    • Green beans
    • Hot dog
