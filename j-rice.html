How to prepare:

  1. Fetch rice of 4 cups of empty 150g thin ..
  2. Wash the rice with cold water and drain.
  3. Get a clean pot,place on heat add 1.5 liter water and add rice , allow to boil for 25mins parboiling.
  4. Wash again with cold water and drain rice put back on heat with little salt ( 1.05 tea spoons ) and 750ml hot water allow to boil until the water is dried for 20 more min.
  5. Remove from fire and pour in a basket to drain
  6. Make a red base sauce : Blend ; Red hot pepper 20 ounces Tomatoes 3 ounces Bell or cayenne 3 ounces 1 red Onions
    7. Drain the water with tiny kitchen filter net Place pot on heat add 750g vegetable oil to heat Toast shopped onion 1/2 ball Add the blended pepper Add chicken sauce water of about 400ml ( optional) Add seasoning to taste Leave for about 5 mins Add water of about 700ml Add bayleaf Add seasoning , salt , thyme to taste Allow it to boil to hundred degrees Pour your rice and steer evenly Allow to dry Add chopped; ( optional) Carrot Bell pepper Onions Sweet corn Green beans Hot dog
