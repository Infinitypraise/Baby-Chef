Toast Bread (Sandwich)

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Butter or margarine
  • Eggs (boiled or scrambled, optional)
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Tomatoes (sliced)
  • Cucumber (sliced, optional)
  • Lettuce (optional)
  • Sardines or corned beef (optional)
  • Mayonnaise (optional)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

How to prepare:

  1. Spread butter or margarine on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Layer your choice of filling (egg, cheese, tomatoes, cucumber, lettuce, sardines, corned beef, mayonnaise) on one slice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper if desired.
  4. Cover with another slice of bread, buttered side out.
  5. Place the sandwich in a sandwich toaster or grill.
  6. Toast until golden brown and crispy (about 3-5 minutes).
  7. Remove, slice, and serve hot.

Proteins (optional):

  • Eggs
  • Sardines
  • Corned beef
  • Chicken (shredded, optional)
  • Cheese
