Chapman

Ingredients

  • Fanta or orange soda
  • Sprite or lemon-lime soda
  • Grenadine syrup
  • Angostura bitters (optional)
  • Fresh lemon or lime juice
  • Cucumber slices
  • Ice cubes
  • Orange slices (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Fill a large glass or jug with ice cubes.
  2. Add equal parts Fanta and Sprite (or orange and lemon-lime soda).
  3. Pour in a splash of grenadine syrup for color and sweetness.
  4. Add a few drops of Angostura bitters if desired.
  5. Squeeze in fresh lemon or lime juice to taste.
  6. Add cucumber and orange slices for garnish.
  7. Stir well and serve chilled.

Pairs well with:

  • Grilled Chicken
  • Fish
  • Beef
  • Snacks
  • Burger

Also with a combo of (optional):

  • Table water
  • Soft Drinks
  • Banana smoothies
  • Salad
