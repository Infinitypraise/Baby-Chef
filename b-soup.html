Banga Soup

Ingredients

  • Palm nuts (or canned palm nut cream)
  • Beef, goat meat, or assorted meats
  • Stock fish (optional)
  • Dry fish (optional)
  • Periwinkle (optional)
  • Crayfish (ground)
  • Onions
  • Scotch bonnet or chili pepper
  • Banga spice mix (or oburunbebe stick, ataiko, and other local spices)
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Scent leaf or bitterleaf (optional)
  • Water

How to prepare:

  1. Boil palm nuts until soft, then pound to extract the juice. Add water and strain to get palm nut extract. (Skip if using canned palm nut cream.)
  2. Pour palm nut extract into a pot and boil until it thickens and oil begins to float.
  3. Wash and season meat and fish with onions, seasoning cubes, salt, and pepper. Boil until tender and set aside, reserving the stock.
  4. Add cooked meat, stock fish, dry fish, and periwinkle to the boiling palm nut extract.
  5. Add ground crayfish, banga spice mix, and more seasoning to taste.
  6. Simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
  7. Add scent leaf or bitterleaf (if using) and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot.
