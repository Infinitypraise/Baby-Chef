Mixed Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Pineapple
  • Watermelon
  • Apple
  • Orange
  • Grapes
  • Pawpaw (Papaya)
  • Yogurt or milk (optional)
  • Honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes

How to prepare:

  1. Wash all fruits thoroughly and peel as needed.
  2. Cut fruits into small chunks for easy blending.
  3. Place all fruit pieces in a blender.
  4. Add yogurt or milk for creaminess (optional).
  5. Add honey for extra sweetness if desired.
  6. Add ice cubes to make it chilled and refreshing.
  7. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  8. Taste and adjust sweetness or thickness as needed.
  9. Pour into cups and serve immediately.

Proteins (optional):

  • Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Chia seeds
  • Nuts (cashew, almond, walnut)

Also with a combo of (optional):

  • Table water
  • Chapman
  • Banana smoothies
  • Fruit salad
