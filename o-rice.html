Ofada Rice

Ingredients

  • Ofada rice (local unpolished rice)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Pick and wash Ofada rice thoroughly to remove stones and husks.
  2. Place rice in a pot and add enough water to cover.
  3. Boil for 10-15 minutes, then drain and rinse to remove excess starch and strong aroma.
  4. Return rice to the pot, add fresh water and a pinch of salt.
  5. Cook on medium heat until rice is soft and water is absorbed (add more water if needed).
  6. Drain any excess water and fluff rice with a fork.
  7. Serve hot, traditionally with Ayamase (Ofada sauce) or stew.
