Vegetable Salad

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Sweet corn
  • Green peas
  • Boiled eggs
  • Irish potatoes (boiled, optional)
  • Baked beans (optional)
  • Mayonnaise or salad cream

How to prepare:

  1. Wash all vegetables thoroughly under running water.
  2. Slice lettuce, cabbage, carrots, cucumber, and tomatoes into thin strips or cubes.
  3. Boil eggs and potatoes, peel and dice them.
  4. Mix all chopped vegetables, sweet corn, green peas, and baked beans in a large bowl.
  5. Add diced eggs and potatoes.
  6. Add mayonnaise or salad cream and mix gently until well combined.
  7. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes before serving.
  8. Serve cold as a side dish or main meal.

  • Beans
