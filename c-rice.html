Coconut Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Coconut milk (fresh or canned)
  • Coconut oil or vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Scotch bonnet (optional)
  • Bell pepper (optional)
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaf (optional)
  • Carrots (optional)
  • Green peas (optional)
  • Chicken or fish stock (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Wash 4 cups of rice thoroughly with cold water and drain.
  2. In a pot, heat coconut oil or vegetable oil. Add chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add blended scotch bonnet and bell pepper (if using) and fry for a few minutes.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and chicken or fish stock (if using). Add seasoning cubes, salt, thyme, and bay leaf.
  5. Allow the mixture to boil, then add the washed rice. Stir well.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is almost done and the liquid is absorbed.
  7. Add chopped carrots and green peas (if using). Stir gently and cover to steam for a few more minutes.
  8. Once the rice is soft and fluffy, remove from heat.
  9. Serve hot with your choice of protein.
