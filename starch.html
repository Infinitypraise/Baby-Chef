Starch

Ingredients

  • Starch flour (cassava starch)
  • Palm oil
  • Water
  • Potash (a pinch, optional for elasticity)
  • Salt (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Mix starch flour with water in a bowl to form a smooth paste.
  2. Dissolve a pinch of potash in a little water and add to the starch paste (optional).
  3. Pour the mixture into a clean pot and place on medium heat.
  4. Stir continuously with a wooden spatula as it begins to thicken.
  5. Add palm oil gradually while stirring until the starch turns yellow and glossy.
  6. Continue stirring until it becomes stretchy and cooked through (about 5-7 minutes).
  7. Add a pinch of salt if desired, stir well, and remove from heat.
  8. Mold into balls and serve hot.
