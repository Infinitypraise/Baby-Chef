Shawarma

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or beef strips
  • Shawarma bread (pita or tortilla wraps)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Cucumber
  • Spices (curry, thyme, paprika, black pepper, chili powder)
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Sausage (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Slice chicken or beef into thin strips.
  2. Marinate with spices, salt, seasoning cubes, garlic, and a little oil for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, add onions and marinated meat. Stir-fry until cooked and slightly browned.
  4. Slice cabbage, carrots, and cucumber into thin strips.
  5. Mix mayonnaise and ketchup in a bowl to make shawarma sauce.
  6. Warm shawarma bread or tortilla wraps.
  7. Place cooked meat, vegetables, and sausage (if using) on the bread.
  8. Drizzle with shawarma sauce, roll up tightly, and toast in a dry pan or grill for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Serve hot, sliced in halves.
