Pounded Yam

Ingredients

  • Yam tubers
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Peel yam tubers and cut into medium-sized chunks.
  2. Wash yam pieces thoroughly and place in a pot.
  3. Add enough water to cover the yam and boil until soft (about 20-30 minutes).
  4. Drain the water and transfer yam into a mortar.
  5. Pound yam with a pestle until smooth and stretchy. Add a little hot water if needed for softness.
  6. Continue pounding until there are no lumps and the texture is elastic.
  7. Mold into balls and serve hot.
