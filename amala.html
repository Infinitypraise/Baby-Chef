INGREDIENTS
  • Yam flour (Elubo)
  • Water
  • Soup of choice (Ewedu, Gbegiri, or any preferred soup)
    • EQUIPMENTS
  • Pot
  • Wooden spatula (omorogun)
  • Bowl
  • Stove or cooker
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Boil water in a pot until it starts to bubble.
    2. Reduce the heat and gradually add yam flour while stirring continuously with the wooden spatula to avoid lumps.
    3. Keep stirring until the mixture becomes smooth and stretchy.
    4. If too thick, add a little hot water, cover, and allow to steam for a few minutes.
    5. Stir again until smooth and well-cooked.
    6. Serve hot with your preferred soup.
