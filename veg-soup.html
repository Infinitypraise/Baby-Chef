Vegetable Soup (Efo)

Ingredients

  • Fresh green leafy vegetables (Efo Shoko, spinach, ugu, or fluted pumpkin)
  • Palm oil
  • Beef, goat meat, or assorted meats
  • Stock fish (optional)
  • Dry fish (optional)
  • Crayfish (ground)
  • Onions
  • Scotch bonnet or chili pepper
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Locust beans (iru, optional)
  • Water

How to prepare:

  1. Wash and season your meat and fish with onions, seasoning cubes, salt, and pepper. Boil until tender and set aside, reserving the stock.
  2. Wash and chop the vegetables. Set aside.
  3. Heat palm oil in a pot, add chopped onions and locust beans (if using), fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Add ground crayfish and pepper, stir for a minute.
  5. Add the cooked meat, stock fish, and dry fish. Pour in the reserved stock and a little water if needed.
  6. Allow to boil for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the chopped vegetables, stir well, and cook for 3-5 minutes (do not overcook).
  8. Adjust salt and seasoning if needed. Serve hot.
