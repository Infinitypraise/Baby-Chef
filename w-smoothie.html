Watermelon Smoothie

Ingredients

  • Fresh watermelon (seedless, cubed)
  • Yogurt or milk (optional)
  • Honey or sugar (optional)
  • Lime or lemon juice (optional)
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint leaves (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Cut watermelon into cubes and remove seeds.
  2. Place watermelon cubes in a blender.
  3. Add yogurt or milk for creaminess (optional).
  4. Add honey or sugar for extra sweetness if desired.
  5. Add lime or lemon juice and ice cubes.
  6. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  7. Taste and adjust sweetness or thickness as needed.
  8. Pour into cups, garnish with mint leaves (optional), and serve chilled.

Proteins (optional):

  • Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Chia seeds
  • Nuts (cashew, almond, walnut)

Also with a combo of (optional):

  • Table water
  • Chapman
  • Banana smoothies
