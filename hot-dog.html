Hot Dog

Ingredients

  • Hot dog sausages
  • Hot dog buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise (optional)
  • Onions (optional)
  • Relish or pickles (optional)
  • Cheese (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Heat water in a pot and boil hot dog sausages for 3-5 minutes, or grill/fry them until heated through.
  2. Slice hot dog buns lengthwise without cutting all the way through.
  3. Place a hot dog sausage inside each bun.
  4. Add ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise as desired.
  5. Top with chopped onions, relish, pickles, or cheese if you like.
  6. Serve hot with fries or salad.
