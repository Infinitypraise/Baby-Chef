Suyawarma

Ingredients

  • Suya (spicy grilled beef, sliced)
  • Shawarma bread (pita or tortilla wraps)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Cucumber
  • Spices (curry, thyme, paprika, black pepper, chili powder)
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Sausage (optional)
  • Suya spice (yaji, for extra flavor)

How to prepare:

  1. Slice suya into thin strips.
  2. Warm shawarma bread or tortilla wraps.
  3. Slice cabbage, carrots, onions, and cucumber into thin strips.
  4. Mix mayonnaise and ketchup in a bowl to make shawarma sauce.
  5. Place suya, vegetables, and sausage (if using) on the bread.
  6. Sprinkle extra suya spice (yaji) for more flavor.
  7. Drizzle with shawarma sauce, roll up tightly, and toast in a dry pan or grill for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Serve hot, sliced in halves.

Proteins:

  • Chicken suya (optional)
  • Sausage
