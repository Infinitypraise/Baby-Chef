Spaghetti Pasta

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato paste or fresh tomatoes
  • Red bell pepper or cayenne pepper
  • Onions
  • Scotch bonnet (optional)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Chicken or beef stock (optional)
  • Hot dog or sausage (optional)
  • Carrot (optional)
  • Green beans (optional)
  • Sweet corn (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add a pinch of salt and a little oil.
  2. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente (not too soft), about 8-10 minutes.
  3. Drain and set aside.
  4. Blend tomatoes, red bell pepper, scotch bonnet, and onions until smooth.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a pot, add chopped onions and fry until translucent.
  6. Add the blended tomato mixture and fry until the oil floats and sauce thickens.
  7. Add tomato paste (if using), curry powder, thyme, seasoning cubes, and salt. Stir well.
  8. Add chicken or beef stock (if using) and allow to simmer for 5 minutes.
  9. Add chopped carrots, green beans, sweet corn, and sliced hot dog or sausage. Stir and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  10. Add the drained spaghetti to the sauce, mix thoroughly until well coated.
  11. Allow to steam for 2-3 minutes, then serve hot.
