Indomie Noodles

Ingredients

  • Indomie noodles (2-4 packs)
  • Water
  • Indomie seasoning and oil
  • Vegetable oil (optional)
  • Onions
  • Scotch bonnet or chili pepper (optional)
  • Carrots (optional)
  • Green beans (optional)
  • Sweet corn (optional)
  • Eggs (optional)
  • Sausage or hot dog (optional)
  • Chicken, beef, or fish (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Boil 500ml water in a pot.
  2. Add Indomie noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes until soft.
  3. Drain excess water if you prefer dry noodles, or leave some for moist noodles.
  4. Add Indomie seasoning, oil, and stir well.
  5. In a separate pan, heat vegetable oil and saut√© chopped onions, pepper, carrots, green beans, and sweet corn (if using).
  6. Add cooked noodles to the pan and mix with vegetables.
  7. Add sliced sausage, hot dog, or cooked protein if desired.
  8. For egg noodles, scramble eggs in a pan and mix with noodles.
  9. Stir everything together and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  10. Serve hot, garnished with extra vegetables or protein.
