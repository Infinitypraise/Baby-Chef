Iced Watermelon

Ingredients

  • Fresh watermelon
  • Sugar or honey (optional)
  • Lime or lemon juice (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Cut watermelon into small cubes, removing seeds and rind.
  2. Blend the watermelon until smooth. Add sugar/honey and lime/lemon juice if desired.
  3. Pour the blended watermelon into ice cream cups or molds.
  4. Freeze for at least 3-4 hours, or until solid.
  5. Serve in cups like ice cream, optionally garnished with fresh watermelon pieces.

Proteins (optional):

  • Yogurt (swirl in before freezing for creamy texture)
  • Chia seeds
  • Nuts (cashew, almond, walnut)

Also with a combo of (optional):

  • Table water
  • Chapman
  • Banana smoothies
  • Fruit salad
