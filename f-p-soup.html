Fish Pepper Soup

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish (catfish, tilapia, or croaker)
  • Scotch bonnet or chili pepper
  • Pepper soup spice mix
  • Onions
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Scent leaf or basil (optional)
  • Crayfish (optional)
  • Water

How to prepare:

  1. Wash and cut the fish into steaks or desired pieces. Place in a pot.
  2. Add chopped onions, scotch bonnet, seasoning cubes, salt, and pepper soup spice.
  3. Add enough water to cover the fish.
  4. Bring to a gentle boil and cook for 10-15 minutes until the fish is cooked through.
  5. Add crayfish and scent leaf or basil (if using). Simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  7. Serve hot, alone or with yam, plantain, or rice.

Proteins:

  • Fish
  • Snails (optional)
  • Goat meat (optional)
  • Chicken (optional)

Also with a combo of (optional):

  • Boiled yam
  • Boiled plantain
  • White rice
