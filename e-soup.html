Egusi Soup

Ingredients

  • Egusi (melon seeds), ground
  • Palm oil
  • Beef, goat meat, or assorted meats
  • Stock fish (optional)
  • Dry fish (optional)
  • Crayfish, ground
  • Onions
  • Scotch bonnet or chili pepper
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Vegetable leaves (spinach, ugu, bitterleaf, or pumpkin leaves)
  • Water

How to prepare:

  1. Wash and season your meat and fish with onions, seasoning cubes, salt, and pepper. Boil until tender and set aside, reserving the stock.
  2. Heat palm oil in a pot, add chopped onions and fry until translucent.
  3. Add ground egusi and stir-fry for a few minutes until it begins to thicken.
  4. Gradually add the reserved meat stock and water, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  5. Add ground crayfish, pepper, and more seasoning to taste.
  6. Add the cooked meat, stock fish, and dry fish. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Add your washed and chopped vegetable leaves. Stir and cook for another 3-5 minutes.
  8. Adjust salt and seasoning if needed. Serve hot.
