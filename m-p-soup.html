Meat Pepper Soup

Ingredients

  • Goat meat, beef, or assorted meats (cut into pieces)
  • Pepper soup spice mix
  • Scotch bonnet or chili pepper
  • Onions
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Scent leaf or basil (optional)
  • Crayfish (optional)
  • Water

How to prepare:

  1. Wash and cut meat into bite-sized pieces. Place in a pot.
  2. Add chopped onions, seasoning cubes, salt, and scotch bonnet.
  3. Add enough water to cover the meat and boil until tender.
  4. Add pepper soup spice mix and crayfish (if using). Stir well.
  5. Simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
  6. Add scent leaf or basil (if using) and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve hot, alone or with yam, plantain, or rice.

Proteins:

  • Goat meat
  • Beef
  • Assorted meats
  • Chicken (optional)
