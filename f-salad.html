Fruit Salad

Ingredients

  • Pineapple
  • Watermelon
  • Apple
  • Banana
  • Orange
  • Grapes
  • Pawpaw (Papaya)
  • Strawberries (optional)
  • Lemon or lime juice (optional)
  • Honey (optional)
  • Mint leaves (optional)

How to prepare:

  1. Wash all fruits thoroughly under running water.
  2. Peel and cut pineapple, watermelon, apple, banana, orange, pawpaw, and other fruits into bite-sized cubes.
  3. Place all chopped fruits in a large clean bowl.
  4. Add grapes and strawberries (if using).
  5. Drizzle with lemon or lime juice to enhance flavor and prevent browning.
  6. Add honey for extra sweetness if desired.
  7. Toss gently to mix all ingredients evenly.
  8. Garnish with mint leaves (optional).
  9. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes before serving.
  10. Serve cold as a refreshing dessert or snack.

Proteins (optional):

  • Yogurt
  • Chia seeds
  • Nuts (cashew, almond, walnut)

Also with a combo of (optional):

  • Table water
  • Chapman
  • Banana smoothies
  • Vegetable salad
